Meet The Team

Built by people who believe consistency should be easier.

C5 Active is founder-led and shaped by lived experiences around fitness, accountability, and community. The team is building a product that helps people feel more supported in showing up consistently.

Why We Started C5

We saw how often people start strong but lose momentum without social support. C5 exists to bridge that gap by connecting people with realistic accountability, local relevance, and a product designed around follow through.
